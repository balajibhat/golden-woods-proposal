The Engine

How a buyer goes from search
to site visit, with no agent.

Six steps. Every one controlled by you. Every lead captured, qualified, and routed to your sales team directly.

Buyer journey: click each step to expand
1

Google Search Ads

Capture buyers actively searching for properties in your area.
Purpose
  • Intercept buyers at the moment of highest intent: when they're searching for property in your area on Google
  • Position Golden Woods as the first result they see, before agents or competitors
  • Drive qualified traffic to a page you own and control
Expected Outcomes
  • Consistent stream of high-intent buyer traffic within 2 weeks of launch
  • Cost per buyer inquiry is a fraction of what you'd pay in agent commission
  • You see exactly which searches bring real buyers, not just clicks
2

Property Landing Page

A dedicated page built for one thing: getting inquiries. Not your main website.
Purpose
  • Give the buyer a focused experience built around one goal: make contact with your team
  • Separate from the main website so it can be tested and improved without touching anything else
  • Present Golden Woods with the quality a buyer expects from a project at this price point
Expected Outcomes
  • Visitors who land here are 3-5x more likely to inquire vs. a generic website
  • Buyers feel confident going direct because the presentation matches the project quality
  • We can test and improve what works without waiting on a web developer
3

Lead Capture

Email for brochure. WhatsApp opt-in. Call booking.
Purpose
  • Turn website visitors into real names with contact details and permission to follow up
  • Offer immediate value (the brochure) in exchange for their information
  • Multiple capture points: email form, WhatsApp button, direct call booking
Expected Outcomes
  • Every visitor gets a clear next step, matched to their preference
  • WhatsApp leads connect with your sales team in minutes, not days
  • You own the lead data. No agent controls who talks to your buyers.
4

CRM + Auto-Routing

Leads enter CRM. Brochure sent. WhatsApp routed instantly.
Purpose
  • No lead sits in an inbox. Brochures go out instantly, WhatsApp inquiries go to the right person immediately.
  • Your sales team gets a notification the moment someone asks to talk, with full context on what they looked at
  • Every buyer is logged by where they came from, what they're interested in, and where they are in the process
Expected Outcomes
  • No manual data entry. Every buyer tracked from first click to site visit.
  • Response time drops from hours to minutes. Buyers don't go cold.
  • You see your pipeline: how many buyers at each stage, who's ready, who needs a push
5

Email Nurture

Drip sequences for leads who haven't acted yet.
Purpose
  • Stay top of mind with buyers who are interested but not ready to act today
  • Share construction updates, inventory changes, project milestones, and social proof
  • Move leads from "interested" to "ready to visit" without manual follow-up
Expected Outcomes
  • Buyers who weren't ready today come back in weeks or months, already warm
  • Your sales team only talks to people who've already seen the project, read the materials, and chosen to reach out
  • Every touchpoint reinforces Golden Woods as a serious, premium project
6

Direct Sale

Qualified buyer books a site visit or connects via WhatsApp.
Purpose
  • This is the point: a buyer who's informed, confident, and talking to your team directly
  • No agent in the middle. No 5-8% off the top. The project sold itself.
Expected Outcomes
  • Each direct sale keeps AED 80,000-360,000 in your business instead of an agent's pocket
  • Buyers arrive already trusting the project. Shorter sales cycle, fewer tire-kickers.
  • You built it. You should keep the margin.
